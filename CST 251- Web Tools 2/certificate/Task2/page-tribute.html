Selena Quintanilla Perez

A women who inspired a whole generation

Here a bit of information about her

  • 1971 on April 16, Selena was born in Lake Jackson, Texas.
  • 1981 At the age of 10 she began performing, she was the singer for her band Selena Y Los Dinos.
  • 1987 Wins best Female Vocalist of the year.
  • 1990 1990 Ven Conmingo was the first Tejano Record to achieve gold Record status.
  • 1992 In 1992 Selena and Chris Perez get married.
  • 1993 Selena wins Grammy award for best Mexican American.
  • 1994 Selena Y Los Dinos plays nemerous tour dates.
  • 1994 Amor Prohibido is released.
  • 1995 Selena performs is what would be her last-live concert at the Astrodome, Houston Texas.
  • 1995 March 30, Selena was shot and killed by the team member of her fan club. She dead in her home town Corpus Christi at the age of 23.
